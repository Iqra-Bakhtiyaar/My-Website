About me Following her dream, Iqra Bakhtiyaar came to the UK in 2014 to pursue a degree in London. For the past 4 years, she has been working really hard to make her dream come true and in 2018 she is a graduate who is considered one of the best students in her batch and one of the most loved colleagues at her workplace.

During her degree, she was found to be very passionate about programming in multiple languages, industrial networking and cryptography. She has also had experience with project management using agile methodologies, quantum computing and formal verification. She loved building logic gates circuits as she used to attend early morning extra drop-in sessions just to satisfy her hunger for learning.

Now she is looking for a graduate role where she can apply her learning and gain new skills. She finds loyalty, genuineness and sense of responsibility to be her unique strengths. In future, Iqra wants to be a cybersecurity analyst/a network engineer besides a professionally qualified programmer. After gaining meaningful experience, she plans to start up her own business in the next 5 years, preferably as a shareholder of a company she works for.
